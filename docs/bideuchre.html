Bid Euchre

Introduction

Welcome to Bid Euchre.

What's Bid Euchre?

Bid Euchre is a variation on the classic game of Euchre played all over the world. It's primarily played in the midwestern United States, and the particular version implemented here in Bloomington, Indiana, USA.

You can find the rules in rules.html.

How to Play Bid Euchre

Using the program should be fairly straightforward, assuming you know how to play our version of Bid Euchre. If not, see rules.html.

Starting a Game

Select New Game from the File menu. You can start a new game even if one has already begun, but you will lose the old game. When starting a new game, it will ask for your name.

Quitting

To quit the program, simply select Exit from the File menu.

Cheat Mode

More of a debugging tool than intended for actual play, but you can select Cheat Mode from the Options menu to enter Cheat Mode. In Cheat Mode, you can see all the other players cards.

Playing against the computer

The game is played with 6 players, 1 human and 5 computer. When it is time for you to do something (play or bid), the display will notify you, either by popping up the bid dialog, or by writing a message in the middle of the display.

Playing with/against friends over the internet

This is not currently implemented. It is planed for a future version.

How to Play

Playing is done by clicking on your cards. Simply click the card you wish to play.

Signal Play

You can send signals to your friends by holding the shift key while you click the mouse on your card. The card will be drawn at a slight angle to indicate it is a signal. Signals are a way of telling partners what suit to lead back. Note that computer players currently do not understand signals and will not play to them.

How to Bid

Bidding is done with the bid dialog. When it pops up, you can select the number of tricks you wish to bid and the suit in which to bid them. Press the Bid button to make a Bid, or the Pass button to pass. If you close the dialog, it will be interpreted as a pass.

In a 'best' bid, you will be given two cards by the AI, then asked to make two discards. You can discard by clicking on your cards, same as playing.
